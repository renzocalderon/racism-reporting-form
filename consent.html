Informed Consent & Data Use

Please read how your information will be used, stored, and protected before you continue. You have the right to decide how your data is used.

Why We Collect This Information

  • Understand the types and frequency of racism, hate, and discrimination in our communities.
  • Offer you appropriate referrals and supports.
  • Inform advocacy efforts and help build stronger, community-based services.

How Your Information Will Be Used

  • Identify trends and understand where support is needed.
  • Include findings in public reports or presentations only in aggregate form.
  • Support advocacy for better services and policies in your area.

How Your Information Will Be Stored

  • Securely in an encrypted system with limited, role-based access.
  • Personal details shared only with your explicit permission.
  • Reports never include names or identifying details.

Will My Information Be Shared?

  • Never shared individually without your consent.
  • Only aggregated, anonymized data may be shared publicly or with trusted partners.
  • You may withdraw or request changes at any time by contacting our team.
